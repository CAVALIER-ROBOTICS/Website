    Meet Our Team

    TWCP Cavalier Robotics, known as CavBots, is a high-energy, student-led FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team from The Woodlands College Park High School located in The Woodlands, Texas.

    FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a robotics community that prepares young people for the future through inclusive, team-based robotics programs for ages 4-18 (PreK-12) that can be facilitated in school or structured afterschool programs. Boosted by a global support system of volunteers, educators, and sponsors that include over 200 of the Fortune 500 companies, teams operate under a signature set of FIRST Core Values to conduct research, fundraise, design, build, and showcase their achievements during annual challenges.

    Our mission extends beyond building robots—we inspire the next generation of engineers, programmers, and problem-solvers. Our team thrives on creativity, teamwork, and hands-on learning, proving that science and technology are not just essential but also thrilling and rewarding.

    Though we are a young team, we've rapidly grown, pushing the boundaries of innovation each season. With every competition, we prove that determination, collaboration, and ingenuity lead to success. The future is ours to engineer—join us on this incredible journey!
