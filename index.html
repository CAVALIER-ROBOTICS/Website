About Us

We are CavBots...

TWCP Cavalier Robotics "CavBots" is a high school robotics team based in The Woodlands, Texas from The Woodlands College Park High School. Each year, we compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition, a nationwide event in which teams of students come together to build a robot that competes in the challenge for that year. CavBots is a student-led FRC team that is committed to providing our members and community with hands-on STEM education. We use robotics as an opportunity to show to students that STEM careers can be enjoyable and rewarding. Although we are a young team, we have grown each season and have shown that we can be triumphant.
